Our Team

What we lack in size, we make up for in experience and efficiency. Our founding principal has nearly a decade of experience a diverse range of project types. From straightforward designs to highly complex challenges, we’ve tackled projects of all sizes with dedication and commitment to success.

Brandon Holcombe, AIA

Principal Architect

Education: Bachelor's of Architecture, Virginia Tech

Brandon, a Delaware native, knew from an early age that he wanted to be an architect. After graduating from Virginia Tech in 2016, he gained experience in a wide range of projects including medical / dental, multi-family, educational, institutional and industrial while working in Nashville, Tennessee. He launched River's Edge Architects in 2024 with the mission to use his experience to help clients realize their vision and to enhance his communities. Outside of the office, Brandon enjoys spending time with his wife, two sons, and two dogs and can often be found golfing or boating.
